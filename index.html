The Lakes

Created naturally by the river carving a new path through the countryside and leaving this beautiful oxbow lake with central island, it creates the perfect habitat for wildlife to live peacefully whilst allowing us to enjoy watching at a safe distance. Egyption Geese, Crested Grebe and Canadian Geese can be seen all year round and if you're lucky enough to spot a flash of blue, you may just catch sight of the Kingfisher as it looks for its next meal. The endangered Water Vole resides on the river banks mingling with the many varieties of ducks dibbling the weed.

Facilities

We have toilets on site, which include a changing area for getting in and out of wet gear. There is no mains water to the club so there are no showers, and all water for the toilets is either rain or lake water. There is a rain water fed hand wash station with soap provided and hand sanitiser is available, however we highly recommend you bring your own too. We have a static caravan which is a members area to use as a dry area only, to warm up and get a wind free view of the lake.

Storage

For our members we have a variety of lockable sheds and containers that offer space for canoes, kayaks and other smaller water sport equipment. We offer outdoor storage for craft under 17ft and outdoor rack space for kayaks, canoes and boards. These are payable annually. Please contact us if you are a member and interested in storing your equipment with us.

Membership

Membership runs from May 1st to April 30th each year. The full fee is payable. All members are asked to agree to and adhere to the club rules and guidelines. Individual Full Membership - £50 per year Family Membership - £80 per year
